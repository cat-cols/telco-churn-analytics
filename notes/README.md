# Telco Churn Retention Analytics
A business-facing customer churn case study using Python, SQL, data quality checks, segmentation, and 
BI-style reporting.

## Business Question
How can a telecom company identify customer segments with higher churn risk and prioritize retention 
actions?

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- SQL
- Matplotlib
- Git/GitHub

## Project Workflow
1. Loaded and profiled raw customer data
2. Cleaned billing and churn fields
3. Created tenure and monthly charge segments
4. Analyzed churn by contract, tenure, payment method, internet service, and charge level
5. Exported summary tables and visuals
6. Developed business recommendations for retention strategy

## Key Findings
- Month-to-month customers had the highest churn risk.
- Short-tenure customers were more likely to churn.
- Electronic check customers showed elevated churn.
- Higher monthly charges were associated with increased churn risk.
- Fiber optic customers showed higher churn than other internet service groups.

## Business Recommendations
- Target month-to-month customers with contract conversion offers.
- Create onboarding interventions for customers in their first 12 months.
- Investigate electronic check payment friction.
- Review pricing and service bundles for high-charge customers.
- Monitor churn risk by contract, tenure, and payment method monthly.
